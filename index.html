Silent Auctions

Auctioneers

  • As an auctioneer, list as many items as you wish. Easily keep track of every listing. You are always able to see and manage every detail about all your listings.
  • Set reserve pricing to ensure your minimum goals are met. Get piece of mind knowing that if the reserve price isn't met, your item isn't sold.
  • You have complete control over how long your items are listed. If an item's reserve is being met, you can choose to extend the time limit rather having to re-list the item.
  • See the bids for your items as they appear in realtime. Always know what others consider your item(s) to be worth.

Bidders

  • Easily browse all available listings.
  • Each item is listed with the most important details easily viewable.
  • Every item has an easily accessible bid button to allow immediately placing a bid.
  • See a listing of all the auctions you have bid on.
